--- 
heading: Pablo(markup)
category: pablo
---


Converts SVG markup into a collection of elements.

    var shapes = Pablo(
        '<rect width="120" height="80"/>' + 
        '<circle cx="150" cy="40" r="30"/>'
    );
    
    Pablo(demoElement)
        .svg({width:180, height:80})
        .append(shapes);


See also the collection method, [`markup()`](/api/markup/).
